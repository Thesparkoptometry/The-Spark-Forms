Save or Print on iPhone

Safari on iPhone doesn't support automatic print dialogs. Here's how to save as PDF or print:

To save as PDF:
1. Tap the Share button (↑ box with arrow) at the bottom of Safari
2. Scroll down and tap "Print"
3. Pinch outward on the preview — this converts it to a PDF
4. Tap the Share button again → "Save to Files"
To print directly:
1. Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari
2. Tap "Print" and select your printer
